# Privacy Policy for LockIn Habit

**Last Updated:** July 5, 2026

## Introduction

LockIn Habit ("the App") is an offline-first habit tracker designed to help users build better habits while respecting their privacy.

## Information We Collect

LockIn Habit does **not** require an account and does **not** collect personal information such as:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Contacts
- Precise location

## Habit Data

All habits, streaks, reminders, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

We do not upload your data to our servers.

## Internet Connection

The core functionality of LockIn Habit works completely offline.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses local notifications stored on your device.

## Analytics

The current version of LockIn Habit does not collect analytics or usage data.

If analytics are introduced in a future release, this Privacy Policy will be updated before they are enabled.

## Third-Party Services

The MVP does not use third-party services that collect personal information.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information because we do not collect it.

## Children's Privacy

LockIn Habit does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Data Security

Your data remains on your device. We recommend securing your device using a passcode or biometric authentication.

## Your Rights

You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will include a new "Last Updated" date.
